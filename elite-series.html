HGL Elite Series

The HGL Elite Series is a Blizzard approved open Hearthstone tournament that rewards players with Hearthstone Championship Tour Points, or HCT Points, for the 2016 Hearthstone World Tour! It's a weekly Hearthstone tournament series that we host every Saturday starting at 4pm EST. The tournament is free to enter and rewards 7 HCT points to the top 4 players, and you can use these points to qualify for the 2016 Hearthstone World Championships where there is over $1,000,000 up for grabs!
