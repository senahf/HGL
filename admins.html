Job Description

Tournament Administrators play a very important role in our organization and represent our company directly through their actions. They assist us in ensuring that all of our tournament operations run smoothly and efficiently to improve the user experience. They are the face of our organization and hold a huge responsibility to withhold our reputation as a company.

Requirements

  • At least 16 years of age
  • Speaks fluent English
  • Owns a working computer and headset/mic
  • Has basic experience and knowledge of tournament administration and management
  • Extensive understanding and knowledge of Hearthstone and its community
